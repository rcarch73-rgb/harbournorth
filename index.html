      Timeline controls

      The starting year defaults to the current year. The final year follows the youngest included person through the planning horizon age.

      Year-by-year timeline

      Ages are calculated as of December 31 for annual planning. Retirement status begins in the calendar year the target age is reached.

      How this component works

      • Creates one row for every projection year.
      • Calculates each included household member’s age.
      • Marks working or retired status from the target retirement age.
      • Attaches retirement, income-start, income-end, and user-entered timeline events.
      • Provides the stable timeline consumed by the future income, spending, tax, and investment engines.
