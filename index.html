      Current engine rules

      • Investment growth uses each account’s expected return.
      • Planned contributions are applied only while their owner is working and only when household cash flow is positive.
      • Annual spending is indexed from the projection start year.
      • One-time income, inheritance, gift, and asset-sale events increase cash flow; expense events reduce it.
      • Deficits are withdrawn in this order: non-registered, cash, TFSA, RRSP/RRIF, FHSA.
      • Real estate and other non-investment assets remain at their entered value. Debt balances remain unchanged in this phase.
      • Income tax is not calculated yet.
